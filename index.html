Welcome

We are a frontline, place-based, community-connected group of organizations rooted in the Coyote Valley bioregion of South Santa Clara County—on the ancestral lands of the Amah Mutsun and Ohlone peoples. We emerged through wildfire, flood, a global pandemic and shared vision, holding space for an ecologically just future through:

    •Sacred Traditional Ecological Knowledge (TEK), Indigenous land stewardship and rematriation
    •Agroecology and regenerative food systems
    •Doughnut Economics and circular economics,
    •Post-capitalist practices
    •Sociocratic governance rooted in community agency •Restorative justice and healing-centered engagement
